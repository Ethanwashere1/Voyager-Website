Make your future yours!

Take the first step in making your future your by downloading our app. Letting you take your future to the stars, one step at a time.

Career Quiz

You'll first be presented with our career quiz. Designed with your interests and personality in mind, it simplifies and destresses the process of picking a career. If you already have a career in mind, you can always skip this step.

Timeline

This takes your choosen career and breaks it down into simple daily tasks that take you from high school to that career. To make it even more personalized, the timeline created for you can be modified to make it that much more tailored to you.

Resource Database

This database has over 350 resources available to students of all career pathways and interests. Featuring internships, volunteering, courses, and summer programs, you'll find something to do that will provide the experiences or teach the skills you need to be successful.

Course Reviewer

This tool pulls an up-to-date list of courses available in your school district or school with course descriptions of the content and reviews from students who have taken the class, giving you a taste of the class and what to expect when your planning what to take for next year or before you even step foot in the classroom.

Resume Reviewer

We know that making a resume is especially hard for high schoolers with so many formats and information on the internet and circulating among friend groups. We make this process easy and simple with a reviewer that will review your resume and provide feedback on what to improve based on strategies that employeers use.

Voyager AI

This chatbot serves as a counselor that available 24/7. No more having to deal with getting ghosted by counselors or having no one to reach out to when you really need that help. Voyager AI will provide tried and true advice and is always there to assist you through the rough patches of your career journey.
